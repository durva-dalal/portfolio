The Work — Case by Case

  • 📧

    Email Strategy Overhaul

    The Problem

    Inherited a flat, one-size-fits-all email programme running at 26% open rate. No segmentation, no testing discipline, content that tried to speak to every student at once and ended up speaking to none of them properly.

    What I Did

    Rebuilt the programme from scratch through MSL. Introduced proper audience segmentation by student type and interest. Implemented subject line A/B testing. Shifted send timing based on engagement data. Wrote tailored content per segment rather than blanket communications.

    26% to 39% open rate · one quarter · sustained
  • 🎪

    On-Site Activations and Events

    The Scope

    Eight monthly on-site activations, each raising awareness across a different pillar of the SU's offering — welfare, sport, societies, student voice, and more. The goal is not just footfall but connection: making students feel genuinely part of something rather than passive recipients of information.

    Beyond the Events

    Vendor sourcing and coordination for SU elections. Post-campaign reporting after every activation and major campaign, feeding data-based insights back into strategy for the next cycle. Full coordination with other SU departments throughout.

    8 activations monthly · data-based reporting · cross-department coordination
  • 👥

    Content Team Management

    The Structure

    Manage a team of 5 student content creators across Instagram, TikTok, and wider digital channels. Each creator is briefed individually — not through blanket briefs — which means every piece of content is considered for its platform and audience rather than repurposed from one to another. All outputs reviewed before publication.

    5 creators · Instagram · TikTok · multi-platform
  • 🖥️

    Campus Digital Screens Programme

    Starting Point: Zero

    The SU had no consistent physical brand presence in high-footfall areas. I identified the gap, proposed the programme, and built the entire system from zero — designing evergreen content assets in Adobe Illustrator, deploying them through Xibo across campus, and creating the content calendar and production workflow so the system could run continuously without starting from scratch each time.

    Campus-wide · Adobe Illustrator + Xibo · built from zero

EMEA Website Redevelopment

How do you rebuild a website across 10+ teams in different countries without losing the thread?

The Brief

ABC Digital needed a full website redevelopment that would serve teams across the EMEA region — different markets, different priorities, one coherent digital presence. The challenge was not just technical or design-led. It was fundamentally a content and coordination challenge: how do you create something that works for everyone without defaulting to the lowest common denominator?

My Role

Responsible for content strategy, information architecture, and UX writing across the entire project. Primary liaison between diverse stakeholder teams and external design agencies — which in practice meant translating between business language, design language, and user needs simultaneously. 30% improvement in user engagement within three months of launch.

Content Strategy · Leam Music Academy

Website Content and Brand Strategy

Single brief: futuristic, informative, captivating. No other guidance provided.

The Brief and My Role

Full website content strategy for a next-gen music teaching platform. The brief was deliberately open — which made it harder, not easier. No UX support, no design brief to follow, no content templates. Sole responsibility for every content decision: layout logic, section hierarchy, how much text each area carries, what the brand voice sounds like for a platform that is both innovative and approachable. The site had to feel like it belonged to a music platform of the future — not a standard course provider dressed up in new fonts.

Outcome

Site launched and is live. All content, hierarchy, and voice decisions remain as delivered — no significant structural revisions required post-launch.
