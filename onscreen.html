Ad Films — Case Files
TV Commercial · 10 FilmsGamezy — IPL Campaign Series2022–2023+
The Brief

India’s IPL season. Six loud competitors. How do you cut through?

Gamezy wanted to convert frustrated rival app users during IPL — India’s noisiest sporting moment. Two seasons. Ten commercials. The brief: be sharper, not louder.

My Role

Lead scriptwriter across both seasons. Campaign concept, all ten scripts, overarching narrative. Worked directly with production to ensure scripts translated on screen.

Watch 1Watch 2Watch 345678910
The Insight

“App Badlo” — Change Your App

When fantasy cricket players lose, they blame the app, not themselves. That is a door. “App Badlo” gave frustrated users a funny, self-aware exit. Comedy as strategy. Ten films, one losing scenario each, all ending the same way: switch the app.

📊Avg 2M+ views per film. Broadcast nationally during IPL across two consecutive seasons.

The Brief and Insight

India’s biggest gifting festival. Make it feel personal.

The truth: brothers are notoriously bad at buying jewellery for sisters. Not because they don’t care — because every sister is different. The campaign reframed Kushal’s as the answer to that anxiety. Script, concept, narrative direction for two films.

Brief: explain iMobile Pay features without a product tutorial. Solution: two Wes Anderson-style short films — symmetrical framing, deadpan delivery. The format earned attention; the message landed in that earned space. Script and creative direction for both.

The Brief

Conservation across 56 forests. Tell it through one tiger.

ICICI Foundation’s CSR spans 56 forests and tiger reserves. The brief: communicate that scale without producing a corporate report.

My Role

Script, casting, costume direction, post-production edits. Anchored through ST9, a female tiger at Sariska — making conservation feel immediate and personal rather than institutional.

Global NGO · Gates FoundationTogether Against TB — Chandraprakash’s Story2023+
The Brief

TB is curable. The brief was about what surrounds it.

For the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation’s India TB-free initiative. Stigma and absence of community support are as damaging as the disease. Show what collective care looks like — without it feeling like a public health poster.

My Role

Script, casting, costume direction, post-production edits. Chandraprakash’s story from Khushipur. Balancing emotional storytelling with factual accuracy and cultural sensitivity.
