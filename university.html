01 — Subko: Beyond the Brew MA Dissertation · 2024
The Brief

An Indian coffee brand wants into the UK

Subko is a specialty coffee roastery founded in Mumbai in 2020. The challenge: the UK coffee market is worth £11.3 billion, dominated by Costa, Starbucks, and Caffe Nero. India is the 8th largest coffee producer globally, yet has almost zero international brand recognition.

The Research

Understand before you create

PESTEL analysis, full competitor mapping, a category map plotting accessibility against sustainability, and a semiotic analysis. Key finding: the gap is high sustainability combined with high accessibility. Every sustainable brand is niche. Every accessible brand has weak sustainability credentials.

Three Creative Routes

Before deciding on direction

Concept 01

“Brewed in Tradition”

Heritage-led. Indian coffee beans from plantation to cup through storytelling and campus pop-ups.

Concept 02

“Sustainability on the Go”

Eco-first. Sustainable sourcing, fair trade, eco-conscious packaging made accessible for students.

Final — “Beyond the Brew”

Desi Brew by Subko

Sub-brand for the UK university market. Custom logotype blending Latin and Devanagari scripts. Earthy palette. Indian folk-art patterns. Packaging, social, campus launch. Slogan: “Soil to Soul.”

02 — O2 Telefonica: Weather Hijack MA Module · 2024
The Brief

Get O2 noticed by people who scroll past everything

The problem: O2’s existing marketing was actively alienating 16 to 30 year olds. Raise awareness without producing another forgettable telecoms ad. Three media channels required.

The Insight

Weather is the only thing everyone shares in real time

Young people tune out static advertising but engage with things relevant to the exact moment they’re in. Weather is the most universally shared real-time experience. Perfect vehicle for a brand whose platform is “Together, Everywhere.”

Three Channels, One Idea

Together, Everywhere

Channel 01

Weather-Reactive Digital OOH

Billboards powered by live weather API. Copy changes automatically based on conditions. Always current. Always relevant.

Channel 02

Social Media Mirror

Time-stamped posts mirroring the exact billboard copy. The brand is present in the same moment as the audience.

Channel 03

Weather Hijack

Secondary executions inside weather apps, transport screens, commuter environments. All copy by me.
